This Privacy Policy describes how PDFConverter ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.
1

Information We Collect

PDFConverter does not require user registration and does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

We may collect the following anonymous information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including features used and time spent.
  • Device Information: Non-personal device data such as device type, operating system version, and language settings.
  • Advertising Data: For ad-supported services, we may collect data to serve personalized ads (this can be disabled in device settings).
2

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze app performance and usage patterns
  • Offer optional in-app purchases and subscriptions
3

Data Sharing

We may share anonymous data with:

  • Advertising partners to serve relevant ads
  • Analytics providers to understand app usage
  • Service providers who assist in app operations
Important: No personally identifiable information is shared with third parties.
4

Children's Privacy

PDFConverter is safe for users aged 12 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 12.

5

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the limited data we collect. Our security practices include encryption of data in transit and secure storage of anonymous usage data.

6

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through app updates and by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

7

Your Choices

You have control over your data and can:

  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device preferences

  • Request information about the data we collect
